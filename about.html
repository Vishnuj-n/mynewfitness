About FitAi

Our mission, values, and the team behind the technology that's transforming fitness

Our Story

FitAi was founded in 2025 by a Student of CMR Institute of Technology who speacializes in LLM & ML specialists who recognized that personalized fitness guidance should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive personal trainers.

We started with a simple question: "How can we make expert fitness guidance available to everyone?" The answer was artificial intelligence that could analyze individual needs and goals to create truly personalized fitness experiences.

Today, FitAi combines scientific research in exercise physiology with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create adaptive fitness plans that evolve as you progress. We're proud to have helped thousands of people transform their fitness journeys and reach goals they once thought impossible.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at FitAi

Personalization

Every fitness journey is unique, and our technology reflects that by creating truly personalized experiences for each user.

Accessibility

Making professional-quality fitness guidance available to everyone, regardless of location or socioeconomic status.

Science-Based

Our recommendations are grounded in exercise science and research, not fads, trends, or untested theories.

Continuous Improvement

We're always learning and evolving, just like our users, by incorporating new research and feedback into our platform.
