Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about FitAi

How do I get started with FitAi?

Simply create an account, complete your fitness profile, and you can immediately start generating personalized workout plans or chatting with our AI assistant.

Is FitAi free to use?

We offer a free basic plan with limited features. Premium plans with advanced personalization and unlimited access start at $9.99/month.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, and your plan will remain active until the end of your billing cycle.

How accurate is the AI fitness assistant?

Our AI is trained on extensive fitness research and best practices. However, it's always recommended to consult with healthcare professionals for personalized medical advice.

Can I integrate FitAi with other fitness apps?

Yes, FitAi integrates with major fitness tracking apps including Apple Health, Google Fit, and Strava to synchronize your activity data.

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry?

Our customer support team typically responds within 24-48 hours on business days. Premium members receive priority support with faster response times.
